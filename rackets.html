🏸 Racket Guide

Choosing the right badminton racket is essential for improving your game. Different rackets are designed for different playing styles. Head-heavy rackets provide more power for smashes, head-light rackets offer faster handling and defense, while balanced rackets give a mix of both control and power. Selecting the correct racket based on your skill level and playing style can significantly improve your performance.
